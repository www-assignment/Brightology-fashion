the ritual

from vision to garment

01

consultation

understanding your story

We spend 2-3 hours understanding your lifestyle, body language, and the statement you want to make. Every crease tells your story.

2-3 hours
02

sketching

digital & hand drawings

Our artists create 3D renders and hand-drawn sketches. You'll see the garment from every angle before a single thread is cut.

1 week
03

pattern making

zero-waste cutting

Using AI-optimized patterns, we cut your fabric with near-zero waste. Every scrap is repurposed for accessories or future designs.

3-5 days
04

couture stitching

hand-finishing

Master tailors spend 40+ hours on hand-stitching. Buttonholes, lapels, and hems are finished with techniques passed down generations.

2-3 weeks
05

final reveal

the fitting ceremony

Your garment is revealed in our private showroom. With champagne and final micro-adjustments, you wear it out the same day.
