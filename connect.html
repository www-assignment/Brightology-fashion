How quickly can I book a consultation?
Typically within 48 hours. Fill the form or call us directly and we’ll find a slot that works for you – virtual or in‑studio.

Absolutely. We work with clients worldwide via zoom, detailed measurement guides, and fabric samples sent to your door.

Our bespoke commissions start from ₦850,000 (or equivalent) depending on fabric, detailing, and complexity. We also offer payment plans.

To give you our full attention, we strictly work by appointment. Please book via the form or call ahead – we’ll prepare tea and fabric swatches.
